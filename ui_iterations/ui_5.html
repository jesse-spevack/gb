Assignment Instructions

Students will design and conduct a controlled experiment to test the effects of different light wavelengths on plant growth. The lab report should include a clear hypothesis, detailed methodology, data collection and analysis, and conclusions supported by evidence. Students must demonstrate understanding of the scientific method and proper experimental design principles.
