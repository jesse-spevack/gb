Instructions
Write a 500-word essay analyzing the themes of love and loss in Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet." Your essay should include specific examples from the text, proper citations, and a clear thesis statement. Focus on how these themes are developed throughout the play and their relevance to modern audiences.

Assignment Summary

Overall, students demonstrated a solid understanding of the themes of love and loss in Romeo and Juliet. The majority of essays showed good analytical thinking and use of textual evidence. However, there are opportunities for improvement in thesis development and essay organization.

Most students successfully identified key scenes that illustrate both themes, particularly the balcony scene and the final tragedy. The connection between the intensity of young love and the magnitude of loss was well-explored across multiple submissions.
